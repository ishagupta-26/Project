    Dr. J. Immanuel Johnraja, HOD

Dr. J. Immanuel Johnraja, M.E., Ph.D is a professor and the Head of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering. He has been the HOD of the department for many consecutive years. He completed his B.E degree from Manonmaniam Sundaranar University in the year 2003 and M.E from Anna University in the year 2005. He pursued and completed his Ph.D from Karunya Institute of Technology and Sciences in the year 2017. Dr. J. Immanuel Johnraja is an expert in the field of Computer Networks, Cryptography and Network Security. He has published many papers in collaboration with other faculties. He is also an instructor in the Cisco Certified Network Professional. Moreover, he has been awarded Advanced Instructor Excellence by Cisco Networking Academy. His extremely friendly and down to earth nature has made all students develop a close bond with him. He is undoubtedly one of the most loved faculties on campus. He is a skilled Professor whose teachings and motivational speeches have had a huge impact on students. His engaging personality and teaching style grabs the attention of all students throughout his lectures. He always encourages students to participate in various events. Students look upto the passion he has for his subject.
